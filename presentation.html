AI Receipt Extraction

Claude Vision reads any receipt format — printed, handwritten, thermal paper.

What It Extracts

  • Merchant name and address
  • Date and time
  • Every line item with raw description
  • Quantity, unit, and unit price
  • Subtotal, tax, and total
  • Payment method

Supported Formats

  • Restaurant Depot receipts
  • Costco Business Center
  • Local wholesaler invoices
  • Any photo (PNG, JPEG, WebP)
  • Auto-compressed for large images
  • ~$0.01-0.03 per receipt

Powered by Claude Sonnet — tuned with a structured prompt for food service supplier receipts. Returns validated JSON with all fields normalized.
